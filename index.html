Facilities

Discover a complete range of world-class facilities designed to elevate your stay beyond expectations. From energizing workouts to rooftop dining, every amenity is crafted with your comfort and satisfaction in mind.

Gym

Stay fit and energized during your stay at our fully equipped fitness center. We provide modern exercise machines, free weights, and personal training services available 24 hours a day.

Swimming Pool

Dive into relaxation at our stunning outdoor swimming pool with a breathtaking city view. Whether you prefer a morning lap or an evening float, our pool is the perfect place to unwind.

Meeting Room

Host your next business meeting or corporate event in our state-of-the-art meeting room. Equipped with high-speed WiFi, projectors, and professional audiovisual systems to ensure your meeting runs seamlessly.

Restaurant

Indulge in a world-class dining experience at our in-house restaurant, offering a diverse menu of local and international cuisine. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to deliver an unforgettable culinary journey.

Rooftop Bar

Elevate your evening with handcrafted cocktails and panoramic city views at our exclusive rooftop bar. The perfect setting to relax, socialize, and watch the sunset in style.

Parking Area

Enjoy peace of mind with our secure and spacious parking area available for all guests. Our 24-hour monitored parking facility ensures your vehicle is always safe throughout your stay.
